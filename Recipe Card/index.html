Burrata & Balsamic Roasted Tomato Crostini
  • Dairy
  • Vegetarian
  • Mediterranean
Oven roasted tomatoes and herbs paired with smooth burrata cheese, served on baked bread
Servings: 2
Prep Time: 30 min

Ingredients:

  • 12 ounces burrata
  • 2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 1 Tbsp Olive oil
  • 1 Tbsp balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tsp honey
  • 1/2 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp chopped fresh thyme
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • salt and pepper
  • chili flakes
  • 1 baguette

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 375 deg F.
  • Slice tomatoes and toss with oil, balsamic vinegar, honey, garlic, basil, thyme, and oregano. Season with salt and pepper.
  • Slice baguette and drizzle with olive oil.
  • Toast bread in hot skillet until golden brown.
  • Spread on fresh burrata cheese.
  • Top with marinated tomato slices. Then bake for 12-15 minutes.
  • Top with parmesan, warm marinara sauce, oregano and chili flakes.
